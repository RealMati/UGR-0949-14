Solar Panel Cleaning Automation

The project focuses on the development of an autonomous robotic system specifically designed for cleaning solar panels. By integrating advanced technologies such as robotics, artificial intelligence, and computer vision, the project aims to create a highly efficient and reliable cleaning solution that can operate in various environmental conditions.

Non-Contact Food Delivery Robot

The non-contact food delivery robot project focuses on creating an autonomous robotic system specifically designed for delivering food orders while minimizing physical contact. By integrating advanced technologies such as robotics, computer vision, and obstacle avoidance, the project aims to provide a reliable and efficient solution for contactless food delivery.
